### reference

| variable | meaning          | unit                           |
| -------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------ |
| $E_p$    | potential energy | $kg \cdot (\frac{m}{s})^2$     |
| $E_k$    | kinetic energy   | $kg \cdot (\frac{m}{s})^2$     |
| $W$      | work             | $\frac{kg \cdot m^2}{s^2} = J$ |
| $P$      | power            | $\frac{kg \cdot m^2}{s^3} = W$ |
